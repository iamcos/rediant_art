Our Collections

Four distinct lines of jewelry, each with its own story

From the signature facechains to flowing bodychains, each piece transforms your presence into living art.

Facechains — The Signature

The facechain is the heart of Rediant. Inspired by tribal face markings and sacred symmetry, it transforms the bridge of the nose into a canvas.

Design Language

  • • Delicate nosepiece sits gracefully, curving like ancient tribal ornaments
  • • Fine chains extend outward, meeting at the ears with symmetrical precision
  • • Subtle details: feathers, geometric patterns, floral silhouettes, crystal drops
  • • Demand attention, draw eyes, provoke conversations

Headchains — The Crown Reimagined

The headchain is a modern crown. Resting on the forehead and hair, it recalls ancient adornments once worn by rulers, shamans, and mystics.

Features

  • • Light, flowing, and radiant with minimal modern lines
  • • Feathers, half-moons, or sacred geometry may hang lightly from chains
  • • Creates movement with every step
  • • Designed to awaken your inner sovereign — subtle, powerful, and magnetic

Bodychains — Flowing Geometry

The bodychain traces the contours of the body, outlining form with elegant symmetry. Worn over clothing or against skin, they bring a sense of ritual and strength.

Design Elements

  • • Chains: Fine and adjustable, creating layers across the torso, waist, or back
  • • Anchors: Designed to sit comfortably on shoulders, hips, or neckline
  • • Ornaments: Sacred geometry pendants, feather motifs, or minimal metallic elements
  • • Materials: Stainless steel, brass, or silver-plated chain — durable, light, and skin-friendly

Earrings — A Dialogue of Details

Earrings at Rediant are never static. They are made by combining metal parts from diverse sources, each shaped like a story: half-moons, leaves, feathers, suns, or faces.

Characteristics

  • • Asymmetrically balanced, playful yet elegant
  • • Complement the larger chains — or stand alone as delicate sparks of personality
  • • Each pair is a reminder that details matter
  • • Complete the dialogue between self-expression and presence

Ready to Transform Your Presence?

Each Rediant piece shares a unifying thread: born from ancient inspiration, crafted through modern artistry, made to attract connection.
