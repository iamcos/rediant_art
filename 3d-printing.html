Our Current Craft: Lost-Wax Casting

Right now, every Rediant piece is created using the ancient art of lost-wax casting. We start with hand-carved wax models, encase them in ceramic investment, then pour molten metal into the cavity after the wax is burned away. This traditional method gives our jewelry its soulful, handcrafted quality.

1. Wax Modeling

Each piece begins as a hand-carved wax model, shaped with precision and artistic vision.

2. Investment Casting

The wax model is encased in ceramic investment material and heated to burn out the wax.

3. Metal Pouring

Molten metal is poured into the ceramic mold, creating the final piece.

4. Finishing

Each piece is hand-finished, polished, and quality-checked before reaching you.

Project Aurora: Our 3D Printing Vision
