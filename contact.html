Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Rediant jewelry unique?

Rediant jewelry blends ancient tribal inspiration with cutting-edge 3D printing technology. Our facechains, headchains, and bodychains are designed to spark conversations and make you unforgettable.

How do I care for my Rediant jewelry?

Our jewelry is made from high-quality metals and designed for everyday wear. Clean gently with a soft cloth and store in a dry place. Avoid harsh chemicals and excessive moisture.

Do you offer custom designs?

Yes! We love creating custom pieces. Contact us to discuss your vision and we'll work together to bring it to life using our 3D printing technology.

How does the crowdfunding work?

We use transparent BTC wallet funding for specific projects. You can contribute to Project Aurora (3D Printing Studio), Project Solstice (Sacred Geometry Collection), or Project Phoenix (Brand Expansion).

Is Rediant jewelry unisex?

Absolutely! All our jewelry is designed to be unisex and inclusive. We believe self-expression transcends gender boundaries.

When will the shop be available?

We're currently in development phase. Follow our crowdfunding progress and journal updates to be the first to know when select pieces become available.
