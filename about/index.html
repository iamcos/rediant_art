Meet Svetlana

Svetlana ("Sveta") is a young woman with red hair and radiant energy who embodies friendship, joy, care, honesty, and creativity. Her journey began with inspiration from facial ornaments and tribal art discovered during travels in India.

Her personality is non-corporate, sincere, curious, and artistic. She believes that jewelry is sacred adornment and self-expression, creating conversation and attraction that makes the wearer unforgettable.

The name "Rediant" is a wordplay on "radiant" and "red," reflecting both her radiant energy and her distinctive red hair. This personal touch infuses every piece with authentic passion and artistic vision.
